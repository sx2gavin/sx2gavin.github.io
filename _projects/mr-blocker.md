Mr.Blocker is a 2D arcade style game that is fast paced and exciting. You as a player is a cursor that can move around and build instant laser walls. There is a piggy that moves within the play area in straight lines unless being blocked by a wall, then it will change it's direction. There are flying ninja stars all over the place. The objective of this game is to try to keep the piggy alive as long as possible.
